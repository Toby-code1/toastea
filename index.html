Main idea

The menu is the concept: one toastie, many places.

Toastea’s signature board is simple and memorable: Toasts of the World. Every toast is served with pickles, dips and crisps, then styled around cities and flavours.

Food-first visuals

Messy, melted, generous — exactly what people want to see.

The best images here are not polished to death. They show the cheese stretch, the wooden trays, the pickles, the crisps and the warm café tables.

Coffee, tea & cold drinks

Not just toasties — a full café stop.

The drinks menu covers espresso, flat white, latte, chai, dirty chai, hot chocolate, loose tea choices, cold drinks, smoothies and milkshakes.

Coffees

Espresso, Americano, Flat White, Latte, Chai Latte, Cappuccino, Mocha and Hot Chocolate.

Tea selection

Black Tea, Green Tea, Peppermint, Turkish Apple, Blood Orange Marmalade and Earl Grey Blue.

Smoothies & shakes

Mango, Strawberry Banana, Tropical Sunrise, Oreo, Twix, Mars, Snickers and Bounty.

The room

Blue walls, warm bulbs, vintage signs and proper sit-down charm.

The interior has a strong personality: café tables, quote walls, tea shelves, patterned surfaces and warm lighting. The website should lean into that instead of pretending Toastea is a plain modern coffee chain.
